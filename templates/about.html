introduction

I have spent the last couple of years developing voice applications for the Alexa smart speakers

As the picture above illustrates, I am primarily a backend developer who can when pressed be full stack

I author applications for businesses using Amazon's Alexa to notify customers of updates and changes, and utilize both custom applications as well as news style flash briefings to get messages to customers

Having been involved in the AWS eco-system, I've gained valuable exposure to the whole array of microservices including NoSQL databases, serverless deployment tools, and the tools to operate fully in a cloud enviroment.

Utilizing Django and python tasks such as automations and scrapes I am capable of returning fresh content through the voice applications for customer usage

I have also developed other very useful tools for customers using serverless lambda and amazon lex bots connecting contact centers with Amazon Connect as well as utilizing AWS dash buttons for trigger actions for tasks.
